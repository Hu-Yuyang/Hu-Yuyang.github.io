Designed a novel diffusion bridge model that leverages Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR) data to remove clouds from optical satellite imagery. Introduced an attention-based fusion mechanism to effectively integrate cross-modal information, achieving state-of-the-art cloud removal results. Work done at MERL. **Published in IEEE TGRS.**
